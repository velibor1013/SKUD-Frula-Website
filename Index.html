About Us

FRULA

PRESERVING OUR CULTURE THROUGH ARTISTIC EXPRESSION

Serbian Cultural Society Frula is a non-profit performing arts organization established in 2009 with the goal to preserve and share the Serbian culture through song and dance. Since then, Frula's members have been privileged to showcase this culture throughout Canada, the United States, and Serbia through traditional performances known as folklor. Folklor is an expression of Serbian heritage, and is considered a very important part of the Serbian culture - shared and passed down for centuries from one generation to another. Many of Frula's performances portray life in various regions of Serbia and tell a story of Serbia's history. A story Frula continues to share locally and internationally!
